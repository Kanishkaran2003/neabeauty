Neobeauty Undereye Clearing Training Landing Page
This repository contains the design and implementation of the Neobeauty Undereye Clearing Training landing page. The project is focused on creating a modern, responsive, and user-friendly interface to promote the training program.

Overview
The landing page is designed to:

Educate users about undereye clearing.
Showcase the benefits and results of the training.
Provide earning potential information for aspiring technicians.
Facilitate easy enrollment for prospective students.
Features
Hero Section:

Eye-catching headline and call-to-action button.
Attractive visuals to draw attention.
Why Undereye Clearing?:

Explanation of the importance of the training with an embedded video.
Before & After Results:

Carousel showcasing transformation examples.
Our Training Approach:

Video explaining the unique teaching methodology.
Earning Potential Section:

Transparent earning breakdown.
Training Contents:

Step-by-step guide to course modules.
Satisfied Students Section:

Testimonials with images of successful students.
Call-to-Action:

Invitation to enroll with a prominent button.
FAQs:

Answers to common questions.
Contact Form:

Simple form for users to get in touch.
